Hi,I am Prachi. I am a designer currently living in Seattle, WA.
Having spent more than a third of my life designing ideas, I also love to travel and meet people. After living on all four coasts of the USA, you can call me the 'Wandering people's designer'! I believe my travels and interactions with different cultures has made me a better designer.

My love for designing was fueled by my mother and grandmother who taught me the value of the 3-Rs. Their teachings, combined with my background in design and technology, I strive to find solutions that create balance between people, planet and profits. I hope to be a learner all my life and hope to continue the legacy I inherited.
